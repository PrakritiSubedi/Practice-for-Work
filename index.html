About the Chancellor Cup

The Chancellor Cup is an annual football tournament featuring teams from various places of Gandaki Province. The tournament is among +2 students of different colleges. The tournament aims to promote sportsmanship and physical fitness among students, as well as to provide a platform for students to showcase their athletic talents. The tournament also serves as an opportunity for students to interact and socialize with their peers from other colleges. The tournament is organized by the Gandaki University Sports Council, which is responsible for arranging the venues, scheduling the matches, and managing the tournament. The council also provides technical support and assistance to the participating teams during the tournament. The Chancellor Cup is a highly anticipated event among students and the local community. The tournament attracts large crowds and generates a lot of excitement and enthusiasm among the participants and spectators. The final match of the tournament is usually held at the university's main stadium and is attended by thousands of people, including students, faculty members, and local residents. The winner of the tournament is awarded the Chancellor Cup trophy and cash prize, and the winning team is honored in a ceremony held at the university. The tournament is also a great opportunity for students to gain recognition and exposure, as many of the participants go on to represent their respective colleges.
